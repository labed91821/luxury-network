Who We Are

Luxury Network is a premier, full-service real estate brokerage located in the heart of Palm Jumeirah, Dubai. Dedicated to providing exceptional experiences within the highly exclusive luxury property market.


The company has in just a few years, cultivated unparalleled relationships with leading developers. This network grants our clients unprecedented access to unique, exclusive deals globally, catering to both discerning personal residences and sophisticated investment opportunities.

"We do not chase transactions. We build legacy portfolios for those who demand the exceptional."

Charles Kadri

Founder & Managing Director


Charles Kadri is a Canadian entrepreneur and the FOUNDER/CEO of Luxury Network Real Estate. He is also the founder of ABITEC Building Contracting, a firm specializing in bespoke ultra-luxury construction with projects in Dubai and Canada. His work has been featured in RIM magazine, which highlighted his commitment to quality in high-end design and construction. A leader in the real estate space, Kadri was nominated for "Remarkable leadership and innovation in real estate brokerage" in 2023. He drives his businesses forward with a focus on meticulous design and strategic real estate development.

Award Winning Excellence

Among the celebrated recipients was Charles K., Founder of ABITEC, who earned the award for Luxury Villas Builder of the Year, acknowledging his firm’s rising reputation for delivering bespoke luxury residences tailored to Dubai’s high-end market.
